Scrambled Eggs:

Eggs are one of my favourite foods. Here is a recipe for deliciosuly rich scrambled eggs.

Ingredients:

  • 2 eggs
  • 1tbs butter
  • 2tbs cream

Method:

  1. Melt butter
  2. Mix eggs and cream
  3. once butter has melted add cream and eggs
  4. Using a spatula fold the eggs every 20 seconds
  5. When eggs are still moist remove from the heat
