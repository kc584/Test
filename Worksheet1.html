The top 5 video games geners are


  1. Action games
  2. Adventure games
  3. Role-playing games
  4. Strategy games
  5. Simulation games

Action genre:

if you like alot of fighting and explosions and weapons then this would be the genre for you

(Some games in this genre include: God of war, Dark souls and Hades)



Adventure genre:

If you like exploring open worlds and finding pieces of lore then the action adventure genre is for you

(Some games in this genre include: Stray, Subnautica andLegend of zelda: BOTW)



Role-playing genre:

if you like immersing yourself into a character and world and playing the way you want then this is the genre for you

(Some games in this genre include: Skyrim, Witcher 3 and Cyberpunk)



Strategy genre:

If you enjoy coming up with clever solutions to beat your enemies then strategy games are for you

(Some games in this genre include: Civilisation, GWENT: The Card Game and Crusader Kings 2)



Simulation games:

If you enjoy doing real life activitys in video games

from the more extreme such as building rockets and making factories

to tending to a farm then this is the genre for you

(Some games in this genre include: Satisfactory, Stardew valley and Factorio)
